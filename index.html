The four Nagel–Schreckenberg rules, applied each timestep
1
Accelerate If v < vmax, increase speed by 1. Drivers want to go faster.
2
Brake If gap to next car < v, reduce v to the gap. Avoid collision.
3
Randomize With probability p, reduce v by 1. Models human hesitation. This rule alone creates phantom jams.
4
Move Advance each car by v cells. Road is periodic — wraps around.
